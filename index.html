Hi there! Thank you for your interest in my webpage. I am a science enthusiast, who is currently pursuing a PhD in (Pale)Oceanography with Prof. Hezi Gildor at the Institute of Earth Sciences, Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Israel. I graduated with a Master's degree in Physics from the National Institute of Science Education and Reserach, Bhubaneswar in May 2017. During my undergraduate years, I devoted much of my time to nonlinear dynamics and its manifestation in various physical phenomena but my internship at ENS de Lyon motivated me to dive into geophysical fluid dynamics. For more information, you can take a look at my curriculum vitae.
