Summation

A blog maintained by Kaushal Gianchandani

Hi there! It seems that you have decided to click on the blog section on my homepage (although I am not quite sure why would you do so!) and I thank you for that. I LOVE to talk and as you could probably tell from the introductory line, this is going to be a pretty informal one-way conversation between us, unless of course you decide on writing back to me which would be highly appreciated.

I have always been somewhat attached to the idea of having a blog and this is the 4th attempt to realize that idea. 'Summation' seems like an arbitrary choice for the name of a blog. The reason why I chose this name is pretty un-ornamented. In 2015, I was looking for an internship for the coming summer when I encountered Dr. Chew Lock Yue's homepage where he mentions the quote:

The whole is more than the sum of its parts.
The quote has stuck with me ever since. This blog is an attempt to present to you the said sum. A small disclaimer, I am not very proficient with hlml, so the structure and existence of this blog are subject to frequent change. Please bear with me. I shall try to maintain (hopefully) a dated list of enteries sorted as per different categories.
